Dice Game

Rules

  • Choose how many players you would like to play the game.
  • Each player rolls a pair of dice 3 times.
  • For each roll, the player's score is the total value of the 2 dice.
  • If any of the 2 dice comes up as a 1, score for that round for the player is 0.
  • If the player rolls a pair of the same numbers, score for that round will be doubled.
  • After the 3rd roll of the dice, player with the highest score wins.
